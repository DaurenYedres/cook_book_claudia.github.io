Grilled Chicken with Quinoa and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 200g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup sautéed vegetables (zucchini, carrots, bell peppers)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

Season chicken and grill on a stovetop. Serve with cooked quinoa and sautéed vegetables. Season to taste.

Macros

Protein: 40g, Carbs: 25g, Fat: 8g

Garlic Butter Shrimp with Zucchini Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1 tablespoon garlic butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

Sauté shrimp in garlic butter until cooked. Toss zucchini noodles in the same pan until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 10g, Fat: 12g

Beef and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g beef strips
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Procedure

Stir-fry beef in sesame oil until browned. Add vegetables and soy sauce. Cook until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 10g, Fat: 12g

Chicken and Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas)
  • 2 tablespoons curry paste

Procedure

Sauté chicken until browned. Add vegetables, coconut milk, and curry paste. Simmer until cooked. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 14g

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 100g pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Procedure

Cook pasta. Sauté garlic in olive oil, add shrimp, and cook until pink. Stir in lemon juice and pasta. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 10g

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 3 large bell peppers
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds. Cook ground beef in a skillet, then mix with rice and tomato paste. Stuff peppers with the mixture, place in a skillet with a lid, and cook over low heat for 15-20 minutes. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 10g

Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fajita seasoning
  • 4 small tortillas

Procedure

Sauté chicken with fajita seasoning in a skillet. Add peppers and onions and cook until tender. Serve with warm tortillas.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 8g

Vegetarian Lentil Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

Combine lentils, coconut milk, tomatoes, and curry powder in a saucepan. Simmer over medium heat for 15 minutes. Serve over rice.

Macros

Protein: 18g, Carbs: 35g, Fat: 10g

Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Procedure

Pan-fry tofu in sesame oil until golden. Add vegetables and teriyaki sauce, then cook until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 20g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 12g

Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g beef strips
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Cook beef in sesame oil until browned. Add broccoli and soy sauce, then cook until tender. Serve warm.
