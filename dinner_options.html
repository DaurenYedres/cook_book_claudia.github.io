Baked Salmon with Potatoes

A balanced dish of baked salmon served with roasted potatoes and asparagus.

  • 150g salmon fillet
  • 150g baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup steamed asparagus (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (15g)
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Macros:

Protein: 34g, Carbs: 39g, Fat: 24g

Steps:

Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Season salmon with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake potatoes for 20 minutes, then add salmon and bake for 12–15 minutes more. Steam asparagus until tender and serve with salmon and potatoes.

Turkey Burger Bowl

A hearty turkey burger served deconstructed with rice and veggies.

  • 100g turkey patty, cooked
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup sautéed zucchini (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon BBQ sauce (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 26g, Carbs: 54g, Fat: 4g

Steps:

Grill or pan-fry the turkey patty until cooked through. In a bowl, layer the cooked rice, sautéed zucchini, and turkey patty. Drizzle with BBQ sauce. Serve warm.

Vegetarian Chickpea Curry

A creamy and flavorful curry made with chickpeas, coconut milk, and rice.

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (200g)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (240ml)
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 tablespoon curry paste (15g)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets (100g)

Macros:

Protein: 23g, Carbs: 105g, Fat: 25g

Steps:

Heat curry paste in a skillet. Add coconut milk and stir well. Add chickpeas and broccoli, then simmer for 10–12 minutes. Serve curry over cooked rice and enjoy warm.

Grilled Chicken with Veggies

A wholesome meal featuring grilled chicken with roasted sweet potatoes and carrots.

  • 150g grilled chicken breast
  • 150g roasted sweet potatoes
  • 100g roasted carrots
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 49g, Carbs: 45g, Fat: 17g

Steps:

Grill chicken breast until fully cooked. Toss sweet potatoes and carrots with olive oil, then roast at 200°C (400°F) for 25 minutes. Serve grilled chicken with roasted vegetables.

Beef Stir-Fry

A savory stir-fry with beef, mixed vegetables, and rice.

  • 100g beef strips
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (15ml)

Macros:

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 54g, Fat: 5g

Steps:

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Stir-fry beef strips until browned, then remove and set aside. Stir-fry mixed vegetables until tender-crisp. Return beef to the skillet and add soy sauce. Serve over cooked rice.
