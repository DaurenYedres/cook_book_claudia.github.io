Chicken Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar salad topped with grilled chicken.

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce (80g), chopped
  • ¼ cup croutons (15g)
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free Caesar dressing (30g)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon grated dairy-free cheese (10g)

Macros:

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 8g

Steps:

Slice grilled chicken breast into strips. In a bowl, combine romaine lettuce, croutons, and optional cheese. Top with chicken strips, drizzle with Caesar dressing, and toss gently. Serve immediately.

Turkey or Beef Wrap

A protein-packed wrap filled with vegetables and creamy dressing.

  • 100g ground turkey or beef, cooked
  • 1 large tortilla wrap (50g)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (lettuce, tomato, cucumber, 80g)
  • 1 tablespoon hummus or dairy-free mayo (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Cook ground turkey or beef until browned. Spread hummus or mayo on the tortilla. Add cooked protein and vegetables. Fold sides of the tortilla and roll tightly. Slice in half and serve.

Lentil & Rice Bowl

A hearty bowl of lentils and rice garnished with sautéed spinach.

  • 1 cup cooked lentils (200g)
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup sautéed spinach (100g)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon olive oil (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 20g, Carbs: 70g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Combine cooked lentils, rice, and sautéed spinach in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil for added flavor. Mix gently and serve warm.

Shrimp & Veggie Stir-Fry

A quick stir-fry with shrimp and colorful vegetables served over rice.

  • 150g shrimp
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 50g, Fat: 12g

Steps:

Stir-fry shrimp in olive oil until pink. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp. Serve over cooked rice.

Chicken & Pasta Salad

A refreshing pasta salad combined with grilled chicken and veggies.

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked pasta (150g)
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil dressing (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 55g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Combine cooked pasta, grilled chicken, and chopped vegetables in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil dressing and toss well. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
