Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Procedure

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.

Macros

Protein: 4g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 20g

Dairy-Free Banana Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: dark chocolate chips

Procedure

Blend bananas, almond butter, and vanilla until creamy. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture. Add chocolate chips if desired.

Macros

Protein: 3g, Carbs: 22g, Fat: 6g

No-Bake Energy Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: dark chocolate chips

Procedure

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Form into small balls and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Macros

Protein: 6g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 8g

Chia Seed Pudding

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: fresh fruit for topping

Procedure

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight until thickened. Serve with fresh fruit if desired.

Macros

Protein: 5g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 8g
