Chocolate Avocado Mousse

A creamy and rich dessert packed with protein and healthy fats.

  • 1 ripe avocado (150g)
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (20g)
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder (30g)
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk (30ml)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Macros:

Protein: 20g, Carbs: 12g, Fat: 16g

Steps:

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Adjust sweetness with stevia or honey if desired. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.

Protein Berry Parfait

A layered parfait with berries, yogurt, and protein for a refreshing treat.

  • 1 cup low-fat Greek yogurt (200g)
  • ½ cup mixed berries (75g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (30g)
  • 2 tablespoons granola (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 18g, Fat: 4g

Steps:

Mix protein powder with yogurt. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in a glass. Repeat layers and serve chilled.

Peanut Butter Energy Bites

High-protein energy bites perfect for a quick dessert or snack.

  • 1 cup oats (90g)
  • ½ cup peanut butter (120g)
  • 2 tablespoons honey (30g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (30g)
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds (20g)

Macros (per bite, makes 12):

Protein: 4g, Carbs: 8g, Fat: 5g

Steps:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Roll into 12 small balls. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

Coconut Chia Pudding

A creamy, high-protein pudding with the goodness of chia seeds.

  • 1 cup coconut milk (240ml)
  • 3 tablespoons chia seeds (45g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (30g)
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup (optional)

Macros:

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 12g

Steps:

Mix all ingredients in a jar. Stir well and refrigerate overnight. Serve topped with fruit if desired.

Baked Apple with Cinnamon

A warm dessert featuring spiced baked apples with added protein.

  • 1 large apple (200g), cored
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter (16g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (30g)
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Macros:

Protein: 15g, Carbs: 25g, Fat: 5g

Steps:

Core the apple and stuff with almond butter, protein powder, and honey. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20 minutes. Serve warm.
