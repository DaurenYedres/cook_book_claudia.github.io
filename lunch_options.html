Ground Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Procedure

Cook ground beef in a skillet. Add vegetables, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Cook until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 12g

Korean-Inspired Ground Beef Bowls

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang sauce
  • 1 cup steamed vegetables

Procedure

Cook ground beef with gochujang sauce. Serve over rice with steamed vegetables. Garnish with sesame seeds.

Macros

Protein: 28g, Carbs: 45g, Fat: 10g

Healthy Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • ½ cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 tablespoons sweet and sour sauce

Procedure

Cook chicken in a skillet. Add bell pepper and pineapple, then stir in sweet and sour sauce. Cook until heated through. Serve with rice or noodles.

Macros

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 5g

Cheesy Chicken Skillet with Broccoli and Rice

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese

Procedure

Cook chicken in a skillet. Add broccoli and rice. Stir in cheese until melted. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 35g, Fat: 12g

Teriyaki Salmon Bowls

Ingredients

  • 200g salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup steamed vegetables

Procedure

Grill or pan-fry salmon. Brush with teriyaki sauce. Serve over rice with steamed vegetables.

Macros

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 15g

Tuna Salad Wraps

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna in water, drained (150g)
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1 cup mixed greens

Procedure

Mix tuna with Greek yogurt. Spread on tortilla, add greens, and wrap tightly. Serve cold.

Macros

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 5g

Tofu and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Procedure

Pan-fry tofu until golden. Add vegetables, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Cook until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 20g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 12g

Egg Roll in a Bowl

Ingredients

  • 200g ground pork or turkey
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Procedure

Cook ground meat in a skillet. Add cabbage, carrots, and soy sauce. Stir-fry until tender. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 15g, Fat: 12g

Asian Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 200g shredded chicken breast
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 2 tablespoons sesame dressing

Procedure

Combine chicken, cabbage, and carrots in a bowl. Toss with sesame dressing. Serve chilled.

Macros

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 8g
