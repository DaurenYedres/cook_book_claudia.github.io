Chicken Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar salad topped with grilled chicken, crunchy croutons, and creamy dressing.

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce (80g), chopped
  • ¼ cup croutons (15g)
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free Caesar dressing (30g)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon grated dairy-free cheese (10g)

Macros:

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 20g, Fat: 8g

Steps:

Slice grilled chicken. Toss lettuce, croutons, and dressing in a bowl. Top with chicken and serve immediately.

Turkey or Beef Wrap

A hearty wrap filled with your choice of turkey or beef, fresh veggies, and a creamy spread.

  • 100g ground turkey or beef, cooked
  • 1 large tortilla wrap (50g)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (lettuce, tomato, cucumber, 80g)
  • 1 tablespoon hummus or dairy-free mayo (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 25g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Cook turkey or beef. Spread hummus on the wrap, layer with protein and veggies, fold, and serve.

Lentil & Rice Bowl

A nutritious bowl combining protein-packed lentils, rice, and sautéed spinach.

  • 1 cup cooked lentils (200g)
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup sautéed spinach (100g)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon olive oil (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 20g, Carbs: 70g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Combine lentils, rice, and spinach in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil if desired. Mix and serve warm.

Shrimp & Veggie Stir-Fry

A quick and delicious stir-fry with tender shrimp and colorful vegetables.

  • 150g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup cooked rice (150g)
  • 1 cup stir-fried vegetables (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (15ml)

Macros:

Protein: 30g, Carbs: 50g, Fat: 12g

Steps:

Cook shrimp in a hot skillet. Add vegetables and soy sauce. Serve over cooked rice.

Chicken & Pasta Salad

A refreshing salad combining grilled chicken, pasta, and a medley of vegetables.

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked pasta (150g)
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (100g)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil dressing (15g)

Macros:

Protein: 35g, Carbs: 55g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Toss cooked pasta, veggies, and dressing in a bowl. Top with grilled chicken and serve.
