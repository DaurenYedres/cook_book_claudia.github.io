Avocado Toast with Eggs

A hearty toast topped with creamy avocado and protein-rich eggs.

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread (70g)
  • ½ ripe avocado (50g)
  • 2 large eggs (100g)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, chopped herbs for garnish

Macros:

Protein: 16g, Carbs: 22g, Fat: 21g

Steps:

Toast the slice of bread until golden brown. Mash the avocado with salt and pepper. Spread avocado on toast. Add cooked eggs and garnish. Serve immediately.

Smoothie Bowl

A vibrant and refreshing bowl packed with fruits and nutrients.

  • 1 banana (120g)
  • ½ cup mixed berries (75g)
  • ½ cup almond milk (120ml)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (15g)
  • Optional: granola, sliced fruits, nuts

Macros:

Protein: 6g, Carbs: 42g, Fat: 9g

Steps:

Blend banana, berries, almond milk, and chia seeds until smooth. Pour into a bowl and add toppings. Serve immediately.

Dairy-Free Yogurt with Toppings

A creamy dairy-free yogurt parfait layered with fresh fruits and granola.

  • 1 cup dairy-free yogurt (150g)
  • ½ cup granola (50g)
  • ½ cup fresh mixed berries (75g)
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional, 5g)

Macros:

Protein: 8g, Carbs: 50g, Fat: 10g

Steps:

Spoon yogurt into a serving bowl. Top with granola and fresh berries. Drizzle with honey, if desired. Serve immediately.

Peanut Butter & Banana Toast

A classic combination of creamy peanut butter and sweet banana on toast.

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread (70g)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter (16g)
  • 1 small banana (100g), sliced
  • Optional: honey drizzle, cinnamon sprinkle

Macros:

Protein: 10g, Carbs: 39g, Fat: 11g

Steps:

Toast the slice of bread to your liking. Spread peanut butter evenly on the toast. Arrange banana slices on top. Optionally, drizzle with honey and sprinkle with cinnamon. Serve immediately.

Oatmeal with Almond Milk & Berries

A warm bowl of oatmeal cooked in almond milk, topped with fresh berries.

  • ½ cup rolled oats (40g)
  • 1 cup almond milk (240ml)
  • ½ cup fresh berries (75g)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (15g)
  • Optional: sweetener of choice

Macros:

Protein: 9g, Carbs: 49g, Fat: 15g

Steps:

In a saucepan, combine rolled oats and almond milk. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until oats are tender and the mixture thickens. Pour the cooked oatmeal into a bowl. Top with fresh berries and chia seeds. Add sweetener if desired. Serve warm.
