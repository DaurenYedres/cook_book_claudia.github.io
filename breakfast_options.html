Avocado Toast with Eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread (70g)
  • ½ ripe avocado (50g)
  • 2 large eggs (100g)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, chopped herbs

Procedure

Toast the slice of bread. Mash the avocado with salt and pepper. Spread avocado on toast. Add cooked eggs and garnish. Serve immediately.

Macros

Protein: 16g, Carbs: 22g, Fat: 21g

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs (150g)
  • 1 tablespoon butter (15g) or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley or chives

Procedure

Whisk the eggs in a bowl with a pinch of salt. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan. Pour in the eggs and stir gently until just set. Sprinkle with pepper and herbs. Serve warm.

Macros

Protein: 18g, Carbs: 1g, Fat: 15g

Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 banana (120g)
  • ½ cup mixed berries (75g)
  • ½ cup almond milk (120ml)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (15g)
  • Optional: granola, sliced fruits, nuts

Procedure

Blend banana, berries, almond milk, and chia seeds until smooth. Pour into a bowl and add toppings. Serve immediately.

Macros

Protein: 6g, Carbs: 42g, Fat: 9g

Oatmeal with Almond Milk & Berries

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats (40g)
  • 1 cup almond milk (240ml)
  • ½ cup fresh berries (75g)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (15g)
  • Optional: sweetener of choice

Procedure

Combine oats and almond milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until thickened. Pour into a bowl, top with berries and chia seeds, and sweeten as desired.

Macros

Protein: 9g, Carbs: 49g, Fat: 15g
