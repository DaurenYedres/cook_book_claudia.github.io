Meal Prep Suggestions

Save time during the week by preparing ingredients and meals in advance. Below are some suggestions based on the weekly grocery lists:

Week 1

  • Chicken Breast: Marinate half the chicken in fajita seasoning for quick cooking. Bake the other half and shred it for use in Chicken Skillet dishes.
  • Bell Peppers: Slice half for fajitas and dice the rest for Stuffed Bell Peppers.
  • Rice: Cook in bulk and portion into containers for Sweet and Sour Chicken and Teriyaki Salmon Bowls.
  • Salmon: Prepare a teriyaki glaze and marinate salmon fillets overnight for easier cooking.

Week 2

  • Pork Loin: Slice into thin strips and season for a quick stir fry with vegetables like broccoli and carrots.
  • Shrimp: Peel, devein, and store in an airtight container. Pre-make a garlic butter sauce for Zucchini Noodles.
  • Ground Turkey: Pre-cook and store in portions to add to Ground Beef Bowls or other quick dishes.
  • Cheddar Cheese: Grate the cheese for quick use in Stuffed Bell Peppers and salads.

Week 3

  • Lamb Chop: Season lamb chops with garlic, salt, and pepper. Pre-sear and store in the fridge for quick reheating.
  • Quinoa: Cook a large batch and store in individual portions for Quinoa Bowls and sides.
  • Tilapia: Pre-season fillets with garlic butter and freeze. Thaw and cook as needed.
  • Greek Yogurt: Portion into containers and top with pre-washed berries and chia seeds for quick snacks.
